Ingredients:

  • Pastry, Shortcrust or puff
  • Sausages
  • Beaten egg

Method

  1. Roll out sheet of Pastry.
  2. Cut rectangles that will wrap the sausages.
  3. Wrap sausages in pastry.
  4. Brush rolls with beaten egg.
  5. Bake in oven till sausages are cooked and pastry is golden.
